Roblox Is Selling Your Data

Posted on Fri, Jan 16th

If you are over 18, Roblox is selling your data.

Roblox automatically turns on "Data Selling" when you turn 18
How to turn it off:
Go into Roblox settings, Head to Privacy & Content restrictions, then head to Ads Preferences, then TURN OF DATA SELLING I also recommend turning off personalized ads.
